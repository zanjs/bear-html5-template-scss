Golden Odyssey

Golden Odyssey's interior layout sleeps up to 12 guests in 7 rooms, including a master suite, 2 double cabins and 4 twin cabins. It is also capable of carrying up to 33 crew onboard to ensure a relaxed luxury yacht experience.

This yacht is built with Steel hull and Steel/Aluminium superstructure. With a cruising speed of 16 knots, a maximum speed of 18 knots and a range of 9,200nm from her 220,000 litre fuel tanks, Golden Odyssey is the perfect combination of performance and luxury.
